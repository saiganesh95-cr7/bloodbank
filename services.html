Our Services

Blood Collection

We organize regular blood donation drives to collect voluntary donations from healthy individuals.

Blood Testing

We perform thorough testing on donated blood to ensure its safety and identify blood types and any potential infections.

Blood Storage

We have state-of-the-art facilities to store different blood components, maintaining their viability for medical use.

Blood Transfusion

We provide blood and blood products to hospitals for transfusions, supporting various medical treatments and procedures.

Emergency Preparedness

We play a crucial role in emergency situations, ensuring a rapid and adequate supply of blood in times of need.
